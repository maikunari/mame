Compose a warm morning brief that includes:

- Today's weather for {LOCATION} (use web_search or browser if needed)
- The day of the week and date
- If today is 大安 (taian) in the Japanese rokuyō calendar, mention it warmly. Only mention 大安 days, skip the other five types. Use web_search to confirm.
- Daily positive content based on the day of the week (this is the part that should feel like a small gift):
  - Monday: Something beautiful happening in nature right now, anywhere in the world — a flower blooming, a migration, a season turning
  - Tuesday: An ayurvedic or wellness teaching, health tip, breathwork technique, or mindfulness practice
  - Wednesday: A piece of genuinely good news from a faraway country — something hopeful and real

  - Friday: Ayurvedic food wisdom, seasonal eating advice, or a simple healthy recipe idea
  - Saturday: Something beautiful from world culture — art, music, architecture, or a tradition worth knowing about
  - Sunday: A gentle reflection prompt or intention-setting thought for the week ahead

Use web_search to find real, current content. Don't make things up or recycle generic facts.
Remember what you've shared before (use memory tool) and never repeat within a month.
Keep it warm, brief, and personal. This is a daily delivery — always send it, never suppress with ALL_CLEAR.
